About 2D Transforms

CSS 3D Transforms are increasingly supported in current browsers as of late 2011. They are however still experimental, and require vendor specific prefixes in all browsers. For a detailed look at how they work, and things to keep in mind, see our article on CSS3 2D Transforms.
